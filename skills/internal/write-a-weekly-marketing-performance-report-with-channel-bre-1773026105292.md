## Overview

This skill helps you write a weekly marketing performance report with channel breakdown. It is designed for Internal team members and produces output in Structured document with clear sections format.

## Process

- Start with a one-line summary of the week
- List top 3 wins with supporting metrics
- List blockers and owners
- State next week's priorities

## What to Avoid

"Never include revenue figures. Don't use acronyms. Keep it under 300 words."

## Quality Standards

- Output must be clear and appropriate for the intended audience
- Follow the specified format consistently
- Be concise and actionable
